Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2025

1. Introduction

At Shekel Budget App, your privacy is our highest priority. This application is designed to be self-hosted, meaning that all your financial data remains local on your own machine. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal or financial information externally.

2. No Data Collection

Shekel Budget App operates entirely on your local system. All data you enter into the app—including income details, expenses, and recurring schedules—is stored only on your device. No data is sent to remote servers or shared with any third parties.

3. Local Data Storage and Security

Since the app is self-hosted, the security of your data depends on the security measures you have implemented on your machine. We recommend keeping your system updated and following best security practices to safeguard your information.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The app does not use cookies or any tracking technologies to collect information. No usage data or analytics are transmitted externally.

5. Third-Party Services

Shekel Budget App is a standalone application and does not integrate with any third-party services for data collection or analytics. Any additional integrations you choose to add are entirely under your control and should be configured with your own privacy considerations.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect any changes in the application or legal requirements. When updates are made, the effective date at the top of this document will be revised. As an open source, self-hosted solution, you are free to modify the app and its privacy practices to suit your needs.
