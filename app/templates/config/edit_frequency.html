Frequency Tips

Payment frequencies help you set up recurring income and expenses.

Common payment frequencies include:

  • Daily: Every day
  • Weekly: Once per week
  • Biweekly: Every two weeks (26 times per year)
  • Semimonthly: Twice per month (24 times per year)
  • Monthly: Every month (12 times per year)
  • Quarterly: Every three months (4 times per year)
  • Annually: Once per year
Pro Tip: Use a descriptive name and add details in the description to make it clear when payments occur.
