Renders the next `PLAN_WINDOW_PERIODS` (13 ~= 6 months biweekly) pay periods starting at `current_period` as a vertical stack of collapsible cards. Each card's collapsed state shows the period's projected end balance prominently with smaller income / expense / net stats; expanding the card reveals static line-item lists for income and expense. Mutual exclusion is delivered by Bootstrap's `data-bs-parent="#plan-accordion"` -- opening one card closes any other. No custom JS is needed for the open/close behavior. Distinct from "This Period": - This Period focuses on a single pay period with prev/next arrows and full edit affordances (Mark Paid, Edit Amount, Open Full, envelope entries with an add-purchase form). - Plan focuses on the forward horizon: balance trajectory across N periods, no edit affordances, no envelope entries (entries are by design a current / past concept). Tap a card to drill into the line items behind a future balance. Decoupled from URL state: the route's `plan_*` context is built from a fixed window anchored at `current_period` regardless of the URL's `periods` / `offset` params (which the This Period arrow nav can drive to `periods=1`).
