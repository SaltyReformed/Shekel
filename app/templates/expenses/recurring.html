About Recurring Expenses

Recurring expenses help you manage repeating bills and payments. These schedules are used to automatically generate expenses based on the frequency you set.

Examples include:

  • Monthly rent or mortgage payments
  • Utility bills
  • Subscription services
  • Insurance premiums
  • Loan payments
Delete Recurring Expense

Deleting a recurring expense schedule will prevent new expenses from being created. Past expenses that have already been paid will not be affected.

Generate Expenses

This will generate upcoming expense entries based on the frequency set in the schedule. Expenses will be generated starting from the next due date.
